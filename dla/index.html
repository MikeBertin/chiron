Things to notice

Why it branches

New particles almost always hit an outer tip first — the branches shadow the bays behind them, so the inside is starved.

The screening effect

Self-similarity

Zoom into any branch and it looks like a smaller copy of the whole cluster — the same pattern at every scale.

What "fractal" means

It forgets its seed

Point, line, ring or block; sticky or not — the dimension still lands near 1.71. The big-scale shape forgets the details.
